Why Paz

Paz Cottage, a self-catering facility, is situated in one of the most scenic areas of Zimbabwe's Eastern Highlands.

Views of the rolling green hills and valleys charecterise the 15 acres of Paz. Altitudes between 1 800 and 2 593 meters provide cool weather and fresh mountain air, perfect for rest and relaxation.

With its stunning mountainous views, and fauna, Paz can provide the visitor with an unforgettable holiday experience.


Fun at Paz !

Paz Cottage, nestled in unspoiled msasa woodlands, is your partner of choice for memorable mountain views for miles down the valley, bird watching, and scenic bush walks along meandering paths which you can consolidate with unforgettable bush braai experiences.

The stone seats in the garden and in the bush offer unique relaxation in a truly serene environment.

Walking down the valley you, you will hear the sound of a waterfall along a perenial river down the gorge with the water slowing down into natural beautiful swimming pools.

Featured Attractions

Various attractive and interesting places to visit, which include: the National Park; Rhodes Nyanga Hotel and Museum; Montclair Casino Hotel; Pine Tree Inn; Froggy Farm Kiosk; World's View, Mount Nyangani; Nyangombe Falls, Mutarazi Falls and Pungwe Gorge & Falls.
